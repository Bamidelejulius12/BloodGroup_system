Welcome to the Blood Group Prediction System

This intelligent system predicts a person’s blood group by analyzing their fingerprint image using advanced deep learning algorithms.

How to Use the System

Upload a clear, high-resolution image of your fingerprint (preferably thumb or index). The system will analyze the unique ridge patterns to predict your blood group.

What You Should Expect

  • Predicted Blood Group Class (e.g., A+, B−, O+)
  • Prediction Confidence (in % probability)
  • Medical/Biological Name of the Predicted Group
